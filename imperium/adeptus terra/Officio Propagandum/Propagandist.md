Propagandists are Imperial rabble-rousers, who give speeches, paint slogans on walls, spread favourable rumours, and create picts. Some, such as [[Kile Simlex]], work for the Officio Propagandum.

## Necromunda
On [[Necromunda]], they are used by [[gang]]s to increase their reputations and help with recruiting new members. Their actives are seen as law-abiding and Propagandists do not have to worry about the Hive World's [[Palanite Enforcers]], like the law-breaking [[Agitators]] do.
