The **Logos Historica Verita** is a new governmental department of Roboute Guilliman's reformed Imperium. Roboute Guilliman awoke in late **M41** to find that the Imperium's 10,000 year history was catalogued in an extremely unreliable, secretive, and often contradictory fashion. He created the Logos as a means to rectify this problem and create the first true reliable history of the Imperium.

The Logos is made up of hundreds of skilled scholars and historians, hand-picked by Guilliman, and chosen for their more liberal views of historiography. The Logos' objectives and policies stand in stark contrast to the [[Ordo Chronos]], which has turned to [[Chronostrife|violence]] in response to Guilliman's reformations.

As of M42, the Logos is estimated to operate around 500 men and women.
