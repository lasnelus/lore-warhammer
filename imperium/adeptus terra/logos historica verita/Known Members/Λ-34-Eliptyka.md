[[λ-34-Eliptyka]] is a Tech-Priest and a member of the [[Logos Historica Verita]], who serves in [[Indomitus Crusade Fleet Primus]]. A [[Lexmechanic]] seconded from the [[Departmento Munitorum]], her skill in data analysis allowed her to swiftly examine the work of her fellow historitors for omissions or internal contractions. λ-34-Eliptyka habitually wore a scarlet robe over her Historica uniform.
