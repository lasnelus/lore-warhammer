A **Palatine** (or **Sister Palatine**) is an Adepta Sororitas battlefield commander who works under a Canoness to coordinate their Order's forces. Though not as high as a Prioress or Canoness, Palatines are nonetheless experienced and fierce warriors capable of effective leading and inspiring their Battle Sisters.
