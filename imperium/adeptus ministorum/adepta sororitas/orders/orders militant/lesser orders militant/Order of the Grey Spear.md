The **Order of the Grey Spear** is a minor [[Order Militant]] of the Sisters of Battle.

During the [[Indomitus Crusade]], they led a successful counter-attack against apostate forces that threatened the Shrinefort of [[Saint Grel Ap Mhorg]].

The Order is also known to have contributed forces to the invasion of the Traitor Knight World of [[Aranae]], alongside the [[Order of the Argent Shroud]] and [[Order of the Ebon Chalice|Ebon Chalice]].
