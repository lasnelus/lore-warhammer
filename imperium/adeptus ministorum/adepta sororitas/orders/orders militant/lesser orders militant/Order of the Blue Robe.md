The **Order of the Blue Robe** is a minor [[Orders Militant]] of the **Sisters of Battle**. They are part of the [[Convent Sanctorum]] on [[Terra]].

### Known Actions
* [[Second Abonian Genocide]] - Aiding [[Halen Jentz|Inquisitor Halen Jentz]].
* [[Octarius War]]. During the conflict the Sisters came to the defence of the Forge World of [[Sigma-Ulstari]].
* [[Chalnath Expanse Campaign]] - The Order fought to defend the Shrine World of [[Saint's Halt]] from both T'au and Genestealer Cultists.
