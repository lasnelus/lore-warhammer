The **Order of the Crimson Chalice**, is a lesser [[Order Militant]] of the Adepta Sororitas.

### Known Actions
The Order took part in [[War for the Aberrus System|the war]] to save the [[Aberrus System]] from a tendril of Hive Fleet Kraken.
The Order also fought in the [[Nachmund Rift War]].
