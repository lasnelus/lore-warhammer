The **Order of the Glowing Chalice** is a minor [[Order Militant]] of the Sisters of Battle, that are the spiritual descendants of the [[Order of the Ebon Chalice]].

Because of this, the Order is exacting in their standards of faith and martial prowess. They launch attacks during the **Terran Dawn**, when [[Terra]] rises over a war-torn world's horizon. Though they are far too distant for its light to reach them, the Order's Sisters still feel the [[Emperor]]'s illumination falling upon them as they go to battle. The Glowing Chalice is known to have established a chain of sanctuaries across the coreward portion of the [[Vedill Sub-sector]] and draw most of their members from the Schola Progenium on [[Vedill I]]. The Sisters have defended the Sub-sector from multiple Chaos incursions, frequently alongside the [[Tempestus Scions]] of the [[9th Iotan Gorgonnes]].
