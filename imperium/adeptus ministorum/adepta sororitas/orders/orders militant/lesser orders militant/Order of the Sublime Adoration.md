The **Order of the Sublime Adoration** is a minor [[Order Militant]] of the Sisters of Battle.

This Order was one of several minor Orders Militant that took part in the Imperial defence against the [[13th Black Crusade]]. Notably, they rescued the [[188th Cadian]] at [[Crimson Fell]].

The Order later took part in the [[Argovon Campaign]], as part of the [[Task Force XI|Indomitus Crusade's Task Force XI]]. While the war against the Necrons raged, the Sisters of the Sublime Adoration fought the xenos on [[Iaso]].

They also committed forces to the [[Siege of Dharrovar]] during the [[Nachmund Rift War]]. They were known to have fought alongside the Sisters of the [[Order of the Burning Light]] and Imperial Guardsmen of the [[901st Dataggan Dragoons]] and [[905th Dataggan Dragoons]]. In the Imperial retreat from [[Dharrovar]], hundreds of the Sublime Adoration's Sisters were killed by the forces of Chaos after the Guardsmen guarding their flanks were overwhelmed.
