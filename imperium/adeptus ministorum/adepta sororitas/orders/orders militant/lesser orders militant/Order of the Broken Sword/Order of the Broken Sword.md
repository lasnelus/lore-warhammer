The Order of the Broken Sword is a minor militant order within the Adepta Sororitas, who wear the colors of ash, for their power armour, and crimson, for their cloaks and lining. It has missions on most of the worlds in the [[Odoacer System]] and the Order defended them, when the System was invaded by the Word Bearers Chaos Lord, [[Amatnim Ur-Nabas Lash]].
