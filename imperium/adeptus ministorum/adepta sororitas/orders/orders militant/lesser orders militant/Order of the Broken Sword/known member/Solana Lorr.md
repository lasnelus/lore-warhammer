**Solana Lorr** is the [[Canoness]] of the Adepta Sororitas' [[Order of the Broken Sword]]. The Order has missions on most of the worlds in the [[Odoacer System]] and Lorr was stationed on the System's capital world [[Almace]]. There the Canoness served as the right hand for Almace's ruler, [[Eamon|Cardinal Eamon]], and she later defended the world, when it was invaded by the Word Bearers Chaos Lord, [[Amatnim Ur-Nabas Lash]].
