The **Order of the Sanctified Shield** is a lesser [[Order Militant]] of the Adepta Sororitas that is based on the Shrine World [[Enoch]]. The Order also contains a number of Sister Hospitallers, who are peripherally associated with the Sanctified Shield and maintain Enoch's [[St. Deploratus]]' Sanitarium.
