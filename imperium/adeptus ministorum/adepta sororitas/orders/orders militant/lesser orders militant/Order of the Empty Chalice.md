The **Order of the Empty Chalice** is a minor [[Order Militant]] of the Sisters of Battle.

They have forces stationed on a Ecclesiarchy stronghold, that is located on a distant, insignificant world on the edge of nothingness. Among the stronghold is a complex built onto an archipelago of smaller islands that the Empty Chalice controls and the islands are connected to each other via bridges and walkways. They have given an outlying part of the complex to the Adeptus Mechanicus [[Metik|Magos Metik]] to conduct his work, and the Order now rarely ventures there. Is not quite clear what Metik does there, but he seems to have the full sanction of the Order to do so. This work allowed the Magos to resurrect the [[Astor Sabbathiel|Inquisitor Astor Sabbathiel]] in M42, who was believed to have died almost a century earlier.
