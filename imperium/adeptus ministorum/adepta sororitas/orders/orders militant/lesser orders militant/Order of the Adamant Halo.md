The **Order of the Adamant Halo** is a [[Orders Militant|minor Order]] of the Sisters of Battle.

They took part in the [[Chromyd Front]], where they defended [[Okharium]] from the Death Guard Chaos Lord, [[Thraxoplasmox]]'s warhost. The Adamant Halo were among the Imperial forces protecting the vital Ferrumore Prefabricum XIX which, under the guidance of the [[Xanthin Atris|Blood Vipers Captain Xanthin Atris]], was saved from the Death Guard.

The Order later contributed 7 preceptories to the [[Nachmund Rift War]] Some of these forces were among the defenders of [[Sangua Terra]].
