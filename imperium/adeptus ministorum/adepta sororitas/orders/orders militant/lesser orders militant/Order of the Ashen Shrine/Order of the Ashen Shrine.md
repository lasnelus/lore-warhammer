The **Order of the Ashen Shrine** are a [[Orders Militant]] of the Sisters of Battle.

The Ashen Shrine was created when the Order split off from [[Order of the Sacred Rose]]. As such, the Sisters of the Ashen Shrine revere Saint Arabella and they believe that the Emperor spoke through her. To emulate their Matriarch, the Order wields a great number of flamers in battle, believing that the gouts of fire signify the Emperor's holy word. Not only that, but when the Ashen Shrine march to war pyric miracles soon follow. Many are the stories of the Order driving their enemy into volcanic fissures that have torn open on the battlefield, of burning meteorites raining down on the formations of their foes, and of war machines exploding into infernos when fired upon by its Sisters.
