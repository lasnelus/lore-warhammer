The **Order of the Charred Veil** is a minor [[Orders Militant]] of the **Adepta Sororitas** founded on [[Saint Isidore’s Rest]] by the sisters of the [[Order of the Argent Shroud]] after the [[Macharian Heresy]]. 

In addition to their abbey on Saint Isidore’s Rest, a thousand Sisters of Battle of the Order are stationed on the shrine world of [[Macharia]].
