The **Order of the Bleeding Heart** are a minor [[Orders Militant]] of the **Adepta Sororitas**. Their abbey is located on the Shrine World of [[Emiline's Hope]], home to the bones of [[Saint Emiline]].

### Known Actions
The order defended Emiline's Hope from Ork forces under the [[Arch Maniac of Calvera]] in 452.M41.

Forces of the Bleeding Heart under [[Sabathine Gizella|Canoness Preceptor Sabathine Gizella]] are also known to have fought a number of actions against Necrons, including the [[Amarok Crusade]], the razing of a tomb complex on [[Dyhazab IV]] and a boarding action against the flagship of a tomb fleet. Gizella and her Sisters later formed part of [[Task Force XI|Indomitus Crusade Task Force XI]], fighting in the [[Argovon Campaign]].
