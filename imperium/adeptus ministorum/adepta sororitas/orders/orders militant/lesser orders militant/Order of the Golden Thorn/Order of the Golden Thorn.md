The **Order of the Golden Thorn** are a minor [[Order Militant]] of the Sisters of Battle.

Its Sisters were among the Imperial forces that defended the Ecclesiarchy-held [[Vatrica System]] and were led by [[Amalica|Canoness Amalica]]. However, sometime after the Great Rift was created, the System was invaded by the [[Szarekhan Dynasty]] forces of [[Tartorekh|Overlord Tartorekh]]. The Necrons' sudden attack allowed them to overwhelm the Imperial forces defending Vatrica and nearly succeed in claiming the System. Thanks to the self-sacrifice of Amalica and her Sisters, though, an Astropathic distress call for aid was able to be sent out. This was later answered by the [[Black Templars]] of the [[Purgus Crusade]], who now fight the Necrons for the fate of the Vatrica System.
