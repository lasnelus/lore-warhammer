The **Fiery Heart** is a [[Retribution Class Battleship]], that serves the Adepta Sororitas' [[Order of Our Martyred Lady]].

### History
---
In M42, it was part of an Imperial fleet, that was sent to end a Chaos rebellion on the vital Mining World [[Siscia]]. The Fiery Heart would be sent as a vanguard to the world, under the command of [[Oris|Canoness Preceptor Oris]].
