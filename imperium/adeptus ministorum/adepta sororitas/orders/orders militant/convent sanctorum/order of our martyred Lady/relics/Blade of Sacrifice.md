The **Blade of Sacrifice** is a relic [[Power Sword]] owned by the [[Order of Our Martyred Lady]] and it has been wielded by a hundred martyrs.

This is because in its wielder's hour of need, the Blade glows with blinding radiance as it consumes their soul. So empowered, the Blade can carve down even the mightiest foes, at the cost of reducing its wielder to another martyred husk.
