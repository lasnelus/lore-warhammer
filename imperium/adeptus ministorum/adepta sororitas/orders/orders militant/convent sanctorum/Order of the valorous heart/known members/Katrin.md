**Katrin** is a [[Order of the Valorous Heart]] [[Dogmata]], who serves under [[Augustah|Canoness Augustah]].

History
The Canoness' forces are currently training on a Desert World within the three sunned [[Tressis-qoraxba System]]. Katrin is taking part in the 15 day training herself and has continuously marched for 11 days so far. Those Sisters who pass out or give up during the training will feel Katrin's lash, in order to motivate them to correct their failures.
