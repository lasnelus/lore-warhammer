The **Cloak of St. Aspira** is a Holy Relic of the Ecclesiarchy and especially associated with its Orders Militant. A magnificent cloak of velvet and fur, it was blessed within the [[Ecclesiarchal Palace]] on [[Terra|Holy Terra]] itself to ward away the blows of enemies, and was worn by [[Saint Aspira|Canoness Saint Aspira]] of the Order of the Bloody Rose during her liberation of a hundred worlds from the heretic Denescura.

It incorporated a weave of mesh armour, manufactured through a process now forgotten to the Imperium, lightweight and yet strong enough to stop a killing shot.

By M41, it belonged to the reliquary of the Sororitas convent on [[Neva]], and was worn by [[Galatea|Canoness Galatea]] as she led the punitive expedition to raze the city of [[Metis]] and execute the rebellious [[Holt Sherring|Baron Holt Sherring]].

It would also be worn by [[Selena Agna|Canoness Selena Agna]], as she lead the Adepta Sororitas of the Order of the Sacred Rose during the [[Kaurava]] Conflict.
