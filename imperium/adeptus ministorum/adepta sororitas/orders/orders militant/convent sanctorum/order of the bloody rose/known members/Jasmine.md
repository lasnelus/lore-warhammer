**Jasmine** was a [[Canoness]] of the [[Order of the Bloody Rose]], who aided [[Inquisitor Vinculus]] during the [[Vinculus Crusade]].

In the [[Battle of Fire and Blood|final battle of the Crusade]], when the Inquisitor was possessed by a Daemon, the Canoness aided [[Ludoldus|High Marshal Ludoldus]] of the Black Templars in battling it, but was easily cut down.
