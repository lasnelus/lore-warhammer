**Aspira** is a Living Saint and was the eighteenth [[Canoness]] of the [[Order of the Bloody Rose]].

She is best known for leading her order in a War of Faith to liberate almost a hundred worlds from the heretic Tyrant of Denescura with a force of only a thousand warriors.

Her [[imperium/adeptus ministorum/adepta sororitas/orders/orders militant/convent sanctorum/order of the bloody rose/known members/Saint Aspira/relics/Cloak of St. Aspira|cloak of fur and velvet]] is a holy relic of the Adepta Sororitas.
