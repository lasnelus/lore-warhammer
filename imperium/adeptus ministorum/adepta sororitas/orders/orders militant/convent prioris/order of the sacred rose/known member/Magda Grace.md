**Magda Grace** is a [[Canoness]] of the Adepta Sororita's [[Order of the Sacred Rose]].
### History
---
**Canoness Magda Grace** has fought against the uncaring tides of the universe for eighty-three long years. From childhood she has held fast to the belief that the righteous have an inner strength, a core of iron within them that stands untarnished by the twisting tides of fate. She would battle Tyranid forces in the [[Cryptus Campaign]], becoming horribly scared by a Lictor during the fighting. Later she was slain by a Lictor buying time for the evacuation of [[Asphodex]].
