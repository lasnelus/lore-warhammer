**Vasiliki** is an **Adepta Sororitas** [[Dominion|Dominion Superior]] of the [[Order of the Sacred Rose]].

### History
---
During the [[13th Black Crusade]], **Vasiliki** was among [[Aveline Aboyé|Celestian Sacresant Superior Aveline Aboyé]]'s strike force that the [[Rex Bellorum]] transported to the besieged Imperial world [[Struthian Tertius]]. There they fought the [[Cult of the Eightfold Prophet]], which was attacking the [[Shrine of Saint Lycestra of the Nine Wounds]]. Following the Cult's defeat, the [[Rex Bellorum]] received a message for [[Aveline Aboyé|Aboyé]] from the [[Talitha|Canoness Talitha]]. After greeting the Celestian, **Talitha** ordered **Aboyé**'s strike force to immediately join the Adepta Sororitas warhost gathering at [[Orison VIII]].
