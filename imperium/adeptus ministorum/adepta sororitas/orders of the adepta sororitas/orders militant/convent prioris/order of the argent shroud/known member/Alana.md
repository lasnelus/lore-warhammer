**Alana** was a Battle Sister of the [[Order of the Argent Shroud]].

She was amongst those Sisters based out of the [[Temple of the Emperor Ascendant]] in Hive Helsreach on the planet Armageddon. Under the command of [[Sindal|Prioress Sindal]], Alana aided her sisters in defending the Temple against invading orks during the [[Third War for Armageddon]].
