The **Order of the Veiled Mantle** is an [[Orders Famulous|Order Famulous]] of the **Adepta Sororitas** which is noted for having a close relationship with the **Inquisition**. **Inquisitors** often recruit them as part of their retinues, where the Sisters of the Order serve as advisors, diplomats or liaisons for the various forces that serve the Inquisitors.
