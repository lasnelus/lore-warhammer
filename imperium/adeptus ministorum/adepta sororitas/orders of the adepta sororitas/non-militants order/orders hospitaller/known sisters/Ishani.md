**Ishani** is an **Adepta Sororitas** **Hospitaller**, who in **M42** was serving alongside the [[Order of the Valorous Heart]]. She was tending to her Sisters, even as something inhuman hunts the fields near them.

Sister Ishani died as a **Imperial Martyr** while triggering the self destruct system of a agri-bunker when it was overrun by the **Tyranid**.
